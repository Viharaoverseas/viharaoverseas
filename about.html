The Milestone Charter

Refund Milestone Framework

We break down our advisory fees into three distinct financial milestones, backed by a written contract that eliminates client disputes.

Milestone 01

Assessment & Onboarding

Includes document evaluation, academic credential assessment filing (WES/ECA), and language test mapping. Fee is fully allocated to initial labor.

Milestone 02

Skills assessment Filing

Verification and formal submission to skills authorities (ACS, Engineers Australia, VETASSESS). Subject to our **Accreditation Refund Clause**.

Milestone 03

Government Submission

Final filing in immigration pools (Express Entry, EOI) and visa applications. Clear, contract-backed milestones that guard client interest.
