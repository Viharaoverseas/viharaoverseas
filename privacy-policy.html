Bespoke Privacy Charter

Vihara Overseas safeguards your personal credential dossier with the highest protocols of corporate integrity. Read our secure transmission policies and strict data-protection systems.

1. Collecting Sensitive Personal Dossiers

To assess, audit, and package permanent residency and student files, Vihara Overseas collects detailed personal portfolios, which include: academic certificates and ECA reports, employment verify records, pay stubs, language test scoring indices, passports, and civil certificates. This documentation is gathered solely under direct client consent to process the requested consultation milestones.

2. Encryption & Bank-Grade Security

We treat all client document uploads with clinical security. Vihara Overseas' folder structures and assessment records are safeguarded using industry-standard **AES-256 bank-grade encryption protocols**. We prevent unauthorized access, security leakages, or document exposure through active technical parameters and restricted administrative access thresholds.

3. Restricted Legal panel Routing

Unlike bulk agencies that distribute client contacts to aggressive sales staff, Vihara Overseas maintains strict access parameters:

  • Your sensitive files are transmitted exclusively to our vetted independent **RCIC (Canada)** and **MARA (Australia)** immigration practitioners.
  • No credential dossiers or contact parameters are ever sold, leased, rented, or distributed to advertising platforms, generic job placement firms, or corporate third parties.
  • Internal consultation files are restricted strictly to elite senior case managers assigned to your private account.

4. Strict Retention Limits & Deletion Rights

Vihara Overseas stores credential files solely for the active duration of your milestone advisory program. Upon case completion, application finalization, contract termination, or at the explicit written request of the client, Vihara Overseas executes an absolute digital wipe of all personal credential folders within seven (7) business days, leaving no trace in active storage databases.

5. Cookie Policies & Transparent Analytics

Our digital platform `viharaoverseas.in` uses basic session cookies exclusively to manage interactive state (e.g. calculator entries, form fields, and navigation preferences). We do not deploy cross-site tracker scripts or aggressive profiling parameters.
